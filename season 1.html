Season 1: Daphne & Simon

The diamond of the season, Daphne Bridgerton, enters the marriage mart with grace and poise — only to strike a scandalous arrangement with the brooding Duke of Hastings. What begins as a ruse soon ignites into a love neither expected. In a world ruled by reputation, they must decide what truly matters: pride or passion.
